Color Palette from Image

    Drag and Drop image anywhere or Click here

    How to Extract Colors from Image?

    • Drag and drop image from your local system to the canvas above for extracting colors.
    • Dominant colors will be extracted from the image and displayed above.
    • Up to 9 colors from image are displayed along with their HEX value.
    • Click the color block to copy value of color in HEX.
    • In case your browser does not support copying values directly to clipboard via script then you can do a manual copy.
    • Click on the HEX value displayed below the color to get color in other formats such as RGB, HSL or HSV.
    • We do not store any images as everything is done inside your browser.
